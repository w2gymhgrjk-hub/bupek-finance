1. Introduction & Overview

The BUPEK Finance Microfinance Management System is a web-based platform designed specifically for BUPEK Finance Limited. It manages the complete lifecycle of microfinance operations: client registration, loan applications, approvals, disbursements, repayments, overdue collections, and financial reporting.

The system runs in your web browser. You do not need to install any software — just open the web address your administrator provides and log in.

2. Logging In & Account Security

How to Log In

1
Open your web browser and go to the system URL (e.g. http://localhost:3000 or your domain).
2
Enter your Email Address and Password.
3
Click Sign In. You will be taken to the Dashboard.
⚠ Never share your password with anyone, including your manager or the system administrator. The administrator can reset your password without knowing your current one.

Account Lockout

If you enter the wrong password 5 times, your account will be locked for 30 minutes. After 30 minutes, try again. If you need it unlocked sooner, contact your administrator.

Forgot Your Password?

1
Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page.
2
Enter your registered email address and click Send Reset Link.
3
Check your email inbox for a link from BUPEK Finance. The link is valid for 1 hour.
4
Click the link in the email, enter your new password, and click Reset Password.

Changing Your Password

Click your name in the top-right corner → Settings → Password tab. Enter your current password, then your new password twice. Password must be at least 8 characters with an uppercase letter, a number, and a special character.

3. User Roles & Permissions

Every user account has a role that controls what they can see and do. Your role is shown next to your name at the top of the screen.

RoleWhat They Can Do
CEO / Admin Full access to everything: manage users, approve and disburse loans, all reports, audit trail, system settings, SMS templates. Can write off loans and reverse repayments.
Operations Manager Same as CEO except cannot access the audit trail. Can approve and disburse loans. Can manage users and view all branches.
Branch Manager Manages their assigned branch. Can recommend and approve loans within the branch. Can view branch reports. Cannot disburse loans.
Loan Officer Creates client profiles, submits loan applications, recommends loans. Records repayments. Can only see data from their branch.
Collection Officer Views overdue loans, logs collection activities (calls, visits, SMS), records repayments, sends SMS to overdue clients.
Accountant Read-only access to all financial reports (profit, expenses, branch performance, PAR, daily collections). Cannot create or modify any data.

6. Loan Management

The Loan Workflow

1. SUBMITTED → 2. UNDER REVIEW → 3. RECOMMENDED → 4. APPROVED → 5. DISBURSED / ACTIVE

At any stage, a loan can be Rejected with a mandatory reason. Each action is recorded with the name, role, and timestamp of the person who took it.

Submitting a Loan Application

1
Click Loans in the sidebar → + New Loan.
2
Search for and select the Client using the search box.
3
Choose a Loan Product from the dropdown (e.g. "Standard Micro Loan").
4
Enter the Principal Amount, Loan Term, and Purpose.
5
Review the calculated interest, fees, and repayment schedule shown on screen.
6
Click Submit Application. Status becomes PENDING.

Recommending a Loan

Loan Officers, Branch Managers, and Management can recommend loans. Open the loan → click Recommend → enter recommendation notes → confirm.

Approving a Loan

Branch Managers and Management can approve. Open the loan → click Approve → enter any notes → confirm.

⚠ Only Branch Manager and above can approve loans. A Loan Officer cannot approve their own submitted loan.

Disbursing a Loan

Only CEO and Operations Manager can disburse. Open an approved loan → click Disburse → select disbursement date → confirm. This action:

  • Sets the loan status to ACTIVE
  • Generates the full repayment schedule with all installment due dates
  • Records the disbursement date and the officer who disbursed it

Viewing the Repayment Schedule

Open any active loan → Schedule tab. Shows every installment: due date, principal due, interest due, penalty, total due, opening balance, closing balance, and current status (PENDING / PARTIAL / PAID / OVERDUE).

Rejecting a Loan

Open a loan at any pending stage → click Reject → type the reason for rejection (required) → confirm. The client and loan officer can see the rejection reason in the workflow history.

Writing Off a Loan

CEO and Operations Manager only. This is used for loans that are unrecoverable. Open the loan → click Write Off → enter the reason → confirm. The write-off amount is tracked separately for expense reporting.

7. Recording Repayments

Recording a Payment

1
Click Repayments in the sidebar → + Record Payment. (Or use the "Record Payment" quick action on the Dashboard.)
2
Search for the Loan (by loan number or client name).
3
Enter the Amount Received and Payment Date.
4
Select Collection Method: Cash, Mobile Money, Bank Transfer, or Cheque.
5
Enter a Reference Number if applicable (e.g. mobile money transaction ID).
6
Click Record Payment. The system allocates the payment to the earliest unpaid installments and updates the outstanding balance.
✔ A receipt number is generated automatically for every payment. You can print the receipt immediately after recording.

Printing a Receipt

1
After recording a payment, click View Receipt, or go to Repayments → click the receipt number.
2
The receipt shows: receipt number, client name, loan number, branch, payment date, collection method, reference number, principal paid, interest paid, penalty paid, total received, and allocation per installment.
3
Click the Print button (top right) to print or save as PDF.

Reversing a Payment (CEO / OM Only)

If a payment was recorded in error: open the repayment → click Reverse → enter the reason for reversal (required) → confirm. The loan balance is restored. The reversal is permanently recorded in the audit trail.

8. Collections & Overdue Loans

How Loans Become Overdue

Every night at 00:01 (Tanzanian time), the system automatically checks all repayment schedules. Any installment past its due date is marked OVERDUE. The parent loan is marked OVERDUE when any of its installments are overdue. No manual action is needed.

Viewing the Overdue List

Click Collections in the sidebar. The page shows all overdue loans with:

  • Days Overdue badge (colour-coded: yellow <7 days, orange 7-30 days, red 30-90 days, dark red >90 days)
  • Arrears amount (how much is overdue)
  • Outstanding principal balance
  • Last contact date and outcome

Logging a Collection Activity

1
Find the overdue loan in the Collections list → click Log Activity.
2
Select Activity Type: Phone Call, Field Visit, SMS, Email, or Letter.
3
Select Outcome: Paid, Promised, Refused, Unreachable, or Other.
4
Write detailed Notes (required — what was discussed?).
5
If outcome is PROMISED: enter the Promise Date and Promise Amount that the client committed to.
6
Set a Next Follow-Up Date to schedule the next contact.
7
Click Save Activity.

Recording Payment from Overdue List

Click Record Payment on any overdue loan card. This takes you directly to the repayment form with that loan pre-selected.

10. SMS Notifications

Automatic SMS Reminders

The system sends SMS messages automatically every day at 08:00 (Tanzania time):

  • Payment due reminders: sent to clients with payments due in 1, 3, or 7 days
  • Overdue reminders: sent at 08:30 to clients with payments 1, 3, 7, 14, or 30 days overdue
⚠ Automatic SMS only works after the Africa's Talking API key is configured in the system settings. Contact your administrator if SMS is not being sent.

Sending a Manual SMS to One Client

1
Click SMS in the sidebar.
2
Select the client using the search box.
3
Type your message or select a template.
4
Click Send SMS.

Sending Bulk SMS

1
Click SMS → Bulk Send.
2
Select a message template from the dropdown.
3
Click Send to All Active Clients (or filtered group).
4
Confirm the bulk send. Each SMS is logged individually.

Bulk SMS permission is available to Branch Manager level and above.

Viewing SMS Delivery Logs

Click SMS → SMS Logs tab. Shows every SMS sent: recipient phone, message preview, status (Sent / Delivered / Failed), date/time, and provider response. Failed messages show the error reason.

Managing SMS Templates

CEO and Operations Manager can create and edit SMS templates. Go to SMS → Templates tab → + New Template. Templates support placeholders: {{client_name}}, {{loan_no}}, {{amount_due}}, {{due_date}}, {{days_overdue}}.

12. Audit Trail

The audit trail records every action taken in the system: who did what, when, from which IP address, on which record, and what changed. Only the CEO/Admin can view the audit trail.

Go to Audit in the sidebar. You can filter by:

  • Action: search for keywords like "APPROVE", "DISBURSE", "LOGIN", "CREATE"
  • Module: Loans, Clients, Users, Repayments, Collections, SMS, Auth, Branches
  • Date range: from date and to date

The table shows: Date/Time, User name and role, Module (colour-coded), Action (colour-coded), Description of what changed, IP Address of the request.

✔ Use the audit trail to investigate suspicious activity, verify who approved a specific loan, or trace any data change. All records are immutable — they cannot be deleted.

14. Common Questions & Troubleshooting

Q: I can't log in — it says "Account locked"

Your account is temporarily locked due to too many failed login attempts. Wait 30 minutes and try again. Or ask your administrator to unlock your account from the Users page.

Q: I submitted a loan but I can't see the Approve button

Your role may not have approval permission. Only Branch Manager and above can approve loans. Ask your Branch Manager or a manager to approve the loan.

Q: The dashboard shows 0 for everything

This can happen when the system has no data yet, or if you are a Branch-level user with no data in your branch. Check that you are assigned to the correct branch in your user profile. Ask your administrator to verify your branch assignment.

Q: SMS messages are not being sent

The Africa's Talking API key may not be configured yet. Contact your administrator. Check the SMS Logs page for error messages — the logs show exactly why each message failed.

Q: I recorded a repayment with the wrong amount — what do I do?

Do not record another repayment to "correct" it — this will create double entries. Contact your CEO or Operations Manager to reverse the incorrect repayment. After reversal, the correct payment can be recorded.

Q: A client's loan shows as OVERDUE but they paid

The payment may not have been recorded in the system yet. Check the Repayments list. If the payment is there, check the repayment date — if the payment date was after the due date, the loan may still show overdue until the nightly cron updates it (runs at midnight).

Q: How do I find a client if I know their phone number but not their name?

Go to Clients → use the search box. You can search by name, phone number, or National ID.

Q: Can I delete a client or a loan?

No. Microfinance regulations require that all records be preserved. You can deactivate a client (status = INACTIVE or BLACKLISTED) or write off a loan, but records are never deleted from the system.

Q: The screen is blank after logging in

Try refreshing your browser (Ctrl + R or Cmd + R). If the problem persists, clear your browser cache or try a different browser (Chrome is recommended). If it still fails, contact your system administrator — the backend server may be down.

Q: How do I print a loan schedule?

Open the loan → Schedule tab. Use your browser's Print function (Ctrl+P or Cmd+P). Choose "Save as PDF" if you want a digital copy.
